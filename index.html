Meet the team


Jennifer Thompson - Yoga Instructor


Jennifer is a passionate and certified yoga instructor who brings a wealth of experience to Harmony Yoga. With a background in Hatha and Vinyasa yoga, she guides students through a harmonious blend of physical postures and mindful breathing techniques. Jennifer is dedicated to creating a nurturing environment where practitioners of all levels can explore and deepen their practice. Her warm and compassionate teaching style fosters a sense of community and encourages personal growth on and off the mat.


David Rodriguez - Mindfulness Coach


As our resident mindfulness coach, David brings a unique perspective to Harmony Yoga. With a background in meditation and mindfulness practices, David specializes in helping individuals find peace in the present moment. His sessions blend traditional meditation techniques with modern mindfulness approaches, offering a holistic approach to mental well-being. David's calming presence and insightful guidance create a supportive space for our community members to cultivate mindfulness in their daily lives.
